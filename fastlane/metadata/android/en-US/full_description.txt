StickyKeys is an open-source Android keyboard that brings an iOS-level sticker and emoji experience to your device. 

Built with privacy as its absolute core principle, StickyKeys operates entirely on-device with zero telemetry, zero analytics, and zero tracking.

Key Features:
- **Instant Sticker Creation**: Create stickers from your photos using on-device ML segmentation. No cloud processing.
- **GIF/WebP Conversion**: Seamlessly import and trim videos, converting them into optimized animated stickers.
- **Intelligent Keyboard**: A fully customizable typing experience with a self-learning predictive text engine that never leaves your phone.
- **Persistent Clipboard**: Keep track of your copied text history safely and locally.
- **LAN Sharing**: Securely migrate your stickers and dictionary to another device over your local network using ephemeral P2P links.

StickyKeys respects your data and requires no internet permission for core features.
